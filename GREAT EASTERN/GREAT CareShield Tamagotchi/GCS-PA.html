Complement your personal accident coverage with GREAT CareShield for the care you deserve

It’s important to note that disability can strike at any age, disrupting careers, family plans, and other future goals. Whether through illness or accident, the financial impact can derail long‑term plans, making protection even more crucial.


While personal accident coverage supports you in the event of accidental injuries, disability coverage provides cash payouts to lighten your daily living expenses. This safeguards your health and finances, allowing you to focus on recovery.


With GREAT CareShield, you’ll receive up to a lifetime monthly payouts of up to S$5,0003 to support your daily and long-term care expenses. Premiums can be paid using cash or your MediSave funds1.

How GREAT CareShield may benefit you

Little or no upfront cash
GREAT CareShield is payable via MediSave funds1. So you can receive enhanced disability coverage with little or no upfront cash needed.


Payouts start from the inability to perform any Activity of Daily Living2 (ADL)
Receive monthly payouts3 and lump sum Initial Benefit4 to support your daily and long‑term care expenses.


Lifetime coverage
Secure your disability coverage with payouts for up to a lifetime.

Sign up today and enjoy up to 40% off your first-year premium5. Thereafter, enjoy 20% off your subsequent premiums throughout your coverage.
