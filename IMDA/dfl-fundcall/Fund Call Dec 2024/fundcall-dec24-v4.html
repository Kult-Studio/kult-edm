Your project should focus on the following pillars of the Digital for Life movement

Digital Access

Support less proficient and vulnerable groups in accessing and benefiting from digital technology.

Online Safety and Wellness

Equip communities with knowledge about digital risks and opportunities to ensure a safer online experience.

Digital Readiness

Enable Singaporeans to learn digital skills and use emerging technology to enhance their daily lives, work, and leisure.

Digital for Life Fund Projects

Discover how innovative ideas have helped communities and changed lives. Let these stories inspire you to create a future where everyone benefits from going digital.

Igniting Young Minds with Coding and Robotics

Spark by VM Education Pte Ltd

Introducing children from lower-income households to coding and robotics through workshops, the project prepared children for the future by developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills through tackling real-life challenges.

Digital Safety for Persons with Disabilities

Generation: Voice by Mr. Lee Hyung Woon

Through workshops and AI-powered roleplay, Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) learnt to use technology while staying safe online. Digital wellness skills taught are designed to meet their needs, including scam awareness, keeping accounts secure and how to navigate the Internet safely.

Engaging seniors through digital photography

Digital Photography for Seniors
by Jia Ying Community Services Society (JYCSS)

Tapping on seniors' interest in taking photographs and sharing them with their friends and loved ones, the programme enabled seniors to pick up photography skills and build up their confidence in navigating functions on their mobile phones - while forging lasting memories and bonds through excursions.
